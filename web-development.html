Frequently Asked Questions About Web Development

Do you use WordPress or pre-built themes?

+

We offer both custom website development and WordPress solutions based on your business needs. For high-performance and scalable projects, we prefer clean, custom-coded development to ensure better speed, security, and SEO optimization. If required, we also build optimized WordPress websites with lightweight themes and performance enhancements.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and responsive?

+

Yes. Every website we develop follows a mobile-first design approach. We optimize layouts, images, and loading speeds to ensure seamless performance across smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile responsiveness is critical for Google rankings and user experience.

Can I manage my website content after launch?

+

Absolutely. We provide user-friendly CMS solutions that allow you to update text, images, blogs, and products easily. For custom-built websites, we can integrate content management systems or provide admin dashboards for easy updates without technical knowledge.

Is your website development SEO-optimized?

+

Yes. Our development process includes technical SEO implementation such as optimized site structure, fast loading speeds, schema markup, clean URLs, and Core Web Vitals improvements to improve search engine visibility.
