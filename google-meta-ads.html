Frequently Asked Questions About Google & Meta Ads

How quickly can I see results from Google Ads?

+

Google Ads campaigns can generate traffic and leads within days of launch. However, consistent performance optimization typically takes 2–4 weeks to refine targeting, bidding strategies, and conversion tracking. Our performance marketing approach ensures faster ROI while building long-term scalability.

Which platform is better: Google Ads or Meta Ads?

+

Google Ads is ideal for capturing high-intent search traffic, while Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram) are powerful for audience targeting and brand awareness. The best strategy depends on your business goals. As a Chennai-based digital marketing agency, we often combine both platforms for maximum lead generation and ROAS.

What is ROAS in digital advertising?

+

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures how much revenue you earn for every rupee spent on advertising. For example, a 4X ROAS means you generate ₹4 in revenue for every ₹1 invested. Our Google Ads management strategies focus on improving ROAS through data-driven optimization and advanced conversion tracking.

How much should I budget for Google Ads in Chennai?

+

Your Google Ads budget depends on your industry, competition, and business goals. In Chennai, small businesses often start with ₹15,000–₹30,000 per month, while larger enterprises invest significantly more. We help you determine an optimal budget based on your target keywords and expected lead volume.

Do you offer free Google Ads audits?

+

Yes, we offer a free Google Ads audit for businesses currently running campaigns. Our experts analyze your account performance, identify wasted spend, and suggest optimization strategies to improve your ROAS and overall campaign efficiency.
